Hi! I'm Jonan, now in the second year of my PhD at Radboud University under the supervision of [Mairieli Wessel](/people/mairieli).

I am researching how Large Language Models can support software engineering, applying human-centered methods to study developer-AI interactions. I'm interested in how both cognitive and organizational factors impact developers' needs. My goal is to improve inclusion, by better aligning LLM-based programming tools with a diverse range of developers' needs. 

Feel free to reach out if you want to chat!
